Soil & Runoff Calculators

Quick calculators for soil concentration, soil mass loss and runoff volume. Enter values and press calculate. Results now shown clearly below each tool.

Soil Calculators

This panel calculates mass loss, concentration and flow from the other two values.

Formulas: Mass loss = Flow × Concentration • Concentration = Mass loss ÷ Flow • Flow = Mass loss ÷ Concentration

Runoff Calculators

Compute runoff volume, required area, or rainfall intensity from the other two values. A runoff coefficient (0–1) can be applied.

Formulas: Runoff = Area × Intensity × Coefficient (Coefficient defaults to 1 if empty).
  • Units: Flow in m³/hr, Concentration in kg/m³, Mass loss in kg/hr.
  • Rain intensity: 1 mm/hr = 0.001 m/hr (so 10 mm/hr = 0.01 m/hr).
  • If a value is missing you can calculate it from the other two (buttons above).
